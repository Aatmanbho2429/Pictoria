✦ FAQ

Questions about Pictoria

A few things people usually ask before trying our AI image and tile search software.

What is Pictoria?

Pictoria is a desktop image search application that uses on-device AI to find visually similar images and tile designs across your folders, without uploading anything to the cloud.

Can I use Pictoria for tile design search?

Yes. Pictoria is built for designers and tile catalog teams who need to search large image libraries by visual similarity — drop in a reference tile and get ranked matches from your own catalog in seconds.

Does Pictoria's image search work offline?

Yes. All AI processing happens on your device. Your images and tile designs never leave your computer, and search keeps working without an internet connection.

Which file formats does Pictoria support?

Pictoria supports JPG, PNG, TIFF, and professional Adobe formats including PSD and PSB — ideal for design and print workflows.
