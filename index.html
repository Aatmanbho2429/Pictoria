✦ How It Works

Simple as 1 — 2 — 3

No complex setup. No cloud uploads. Just open, search, find.

1

Add Your Folders

Point Pictoria to any folders on your PC. It indexes them once with AI embeddings, then keeps them in sync automatically.

2

Drop or Paste an Image

Choose a reference image, paste one from your clipboard, or open Pictoria instantly with the global hotkey from anywhere.

3

Get Ranked Matches

Pictoria returns the most visually similar images, ranked by similarity score — instantly, right on your machine.
